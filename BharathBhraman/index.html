LOCATIONS

Lakshadweep is a union territory of India. It is an archipelago of 36 islands serving as the maritime boundary between the Arabian Sea to the west and the Laccadive Sea to the east. It is located 200 to 440 km (120 to 270 mi) off the Malabar Coast of India.

EXPERIENCES

Lakshadweep Islands has so much to offer, apart from it's natural beauty and heritage, there is so much cultural knowledge, historical presence and much more to explore.

Agatti Island is an incredible place with calm water, ensuring one has a surreal experience. One looking for the perfect things to do in Agatti Island never misses out to go for snorkelling. The shallow shores are full of colourful reefs and exotic underwater animals.
